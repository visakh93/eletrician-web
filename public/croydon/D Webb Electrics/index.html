What We Do

Electrical Services in Solihull

From minor repairs to full installations — we handle all domestic and small commercial electrical needs with care, safety, and precision.

Electrical Repairs

Quick, safe diagnosis and repair of electrical faults, broken circuits, tripped breakers, and wiring issues throughout your property.

Fault Finding

Systematic electrical fault diagnosis to locate and fix hidden issues — power loss, flickering lights, and intermittent circuit problems.

Consumer Unit Upgrades

Upgrade your old fuse board to a modern, safer consumer unit with RCD protection — essential for any UK home or business.

Lighting Installation

Downlights, LED strip lighting, pendants, outdoor security lights — professionally fitted for great effect and energy efficiency.

Full Rewiring

Complete domestic rewires for older properties — ensuring your home meets current wiring regulations and is safe for years to come.

Socket & Switch Installation

Extra sockets, USB outlets, dimmer switches, and smart switches installed cleanly and safely to modern standards.

Landlord Electrical Checks

EICR (Electrical Installation Condition Reports) for landlords — legally required every 5 years for all rental properties in England.

Maintenance Work

Planned and reactive electrical maintenance for homes and small businesses — keeping your electrics running safely and reliably.
